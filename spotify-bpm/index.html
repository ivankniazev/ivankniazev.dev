Spotify BPM

Released 2025

Overview

Discover the tempo of your favorite music with Spotify BPM, the ultimate tool for analyzing and displaying the beats per minute (BPM) of any Spotify track in real time. Whether you're a DJ, musician, dancer, or just a curious listener, this extension makes it simple to explore and understand the rhythm behind every song.

Technology Stack

JavaScript Chrome Extension API Spotify Web API Deezer API Google Cloud Run Google OAuth Paddle Payment Manifest V3

Key Features

Instant BPM Detection

Get accurate tempo analysis of any Spotify song you play with instant results displayed right in the extension.

Alternative Tempos

See common BPM variations including half-speed, normal, and double-speed to help you find the perfect tempo for your needs.

Automatic Updates

The BPM refreshes automatically as your track changes with no extra clicks required

Free & Premium Plans

Enjoy 3 free BPM analyses per day, or upgrade to Premium for unlimited access to tempo information.

Google Integration

Sign in with your Google account to track usage and manage your Premium subscription seamlessly.

Multi-API Architecture

Uses both Spotify and Deezer APIs with advanced ISRC matching for the most accurate tempo detection.

Perfect For

DJs and Musicians

Quickly find BPM to prepare mixes and practice sessions with tempo-matched tracks.

Dancers and Fitness Trainers

Build playlists with the perfect tempo for choreography, workouts, or dance routines.

Music Enthusiasts

Learn more about the rhythm and structure of your favorite songs and discover new music by tempo.
