Refund Policy

Last updated: January 16, 2025

1. Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds for digital products and services within 30 days of purchase, provided the request meets our refund criteria.

2. Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact us at ivan@ivankniazev.dev with your purchase details and reason for the refund request.

3. Processing Time

Refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days after approval. The refund will be credited to the original payment method used for the purchase.

4. Non-Refundable Items

Certain items may not be eligible for refunds, including:

  • Custom development work that has been completed
  • Services that have been fully delivered
  • Digital products that have been downloaded and used extensively

5. Partial Refunds

In some cases, partial refunds may be granted for services or products that have been partially used or delivered.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions about our Refund Policy, please contact us at ivan@ivankniazev.dev.
